Background

Why Drug Testing Matters

Roadside drug testing helps reduce impaired driving and serious crashes. This site summarises key indicators— total tests, % positive, differences across jurisdictions, and testing stages—so stakeholders can monitor risk and target interventions.

◀ Back Next ▶

Aim & Objectives

Road Safety — Drug Testing tracks multi-year patterns (tests conducted & % positive), compares jurisdictions on population-adjusted rates, and outlines testing stages. Our aim: a friendly, credible snapshot to guide enforcement and education resources.
