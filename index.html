Background

Why Drug Testing Matters

Roadside drug testing plays an essential role in reducing impaired driving. This project explores five key questions: how positive drug detections have changed over time, which jurisdictions show high or low positive rates, which detection methods record the most positives, what enforcement actions are most common, and which age groups are most affected. These insights are derived from official police enforcement data and processed CSV files (Chart 1–5).

Aim & Objectives

Road Safety — Drug Testing Insights aims to present clear, data-driven findings based on five core research questions. Using multi-year drug test data, we identify trends in positive detections (2008–2024), compare jurisdictions, examine detection methods, analyse enforcement actions, and highlight age groups with the highest positive results. All visuals are generated from the cleaned datasets (Chart 1.csv to Chart 5.csv).
